## Long description (plain text)

**歌sync — learn languages through music, with lyrics that follow the song in real time.**

Utasync turns YouTube links or your own audio files into a bilingual practice player. Paste a link or upload a track, sync the lyrics, and study line by line with readings, translations, and tools built for language learners.

Everything runs in your browser. No account required. No monthly fee. Your library, audio, and AI models stay on your device.

### Try before you buy

The free version at [utasync.app](https://utasync.app) includes YouTube playback, synced lyrics, tap-to-sync editing, phonetic readings, and line-by-line seeking.

You also get **2 full Pro trial songs** — pick any two tracks and unlock every Pro feature on those songs permanently, even if you never purchase.

### What Pro unlocks

**Unlimited songs** with the full toolkit on every track:

- **Local audio upload** — study offline with your own files (MP3, FLAC, etc.)
- **AI auto-alignment** — on-device Whisper transcription matches lyrics to audio automatically
- **Vocal separation** — isolate vocals before transcription on capable devices (optional)
- **Speed control** — pitch-preserving slowdown so you can catch every syllable
- **A/B loop + crossfade** — repeat a verse until it sticks; save loop playlists per song
- **Word-level alignment** — color-matched words between Japanese and English
- **Grammar hints** — pattern callouts for Japanese and English lyrics
- **Cloze mode** — hide words on the active line at easy, medium, or hard difficulty
- **Export** — download LRC, SRT, or A/B clip audio with optional subtitles

Japanese support includes tokenization, romaji, furigana, and grammar patterns. English support includes IPA readings and grammar hints. Add a second language via paste, smart line matching, or manual alignment.

### Built for privacy and independence

- **Offline-first PWA** — install on desktop or mobile; works without a backend server
- **On-device AI** — Whisper and alignment models download once and cache locally
- **No ads, no tracking account** — your songs and progress stay in your browser
- **All future updates included** — one purchase, keep forever

### Who it's for

- Japanese learners studying J-pop, anime openings, or city pop
- English learners working through bilingual tracks
- Anyone who learns better by listening and singing along than by drilling flashcards alone

## How to activate (add to product page or confirmation email)

1. Open **[utasync.app](https://utasync.app)** in a modern browser (Chrome, Edge, or Safari recommended).
2. When a Pro feature prompts you — or anytime in **Settings** — choose **Restore License**.
3. Paste the license key from your Payhip receipt email.
4. Pro unlocks immediately. The key is yours to keep; use it again if you switch browsers or devices.

**Tip:** Upload local audio for the best experience. Auto-align, speed control, A/B export, and vocal separation require a file on your device (YouTube-only songs can attach audio later from the player).

## FAQ

**Is this a subscription?**  
No. $9.99 once. Pro is yours permanently on the official app.

**Do I need an account?**  
No. Utasync has no login. Your library lives in your browser.

**Can I use it offline?**  
Yes, after you've opened the app and cached any AI models you need. Upload local audio for full offline playback.

**What browsers are supported?**  
Chrome and Edge work best (WebGPU for AI features). Safari is supported; Firefox has limited AI support. See the app for your device's tier (Full, Lite, or Manual).

**I already used my 2 trial songs. Can I try more?**  
Trials are per-browser. Pro removes the limit entirely.

**I lost my license key.**  
Check your Payhip purchase email or log into Payhip → My Account → Purchases. You can restore the same key on any device.

**Can I get a refund?**  
Contact the seller through Payhip. Please try the free version and 2 trial songs before purchasing.

**Is the source code included?**  
No — this is a Pro license for the hosted app. The project is open source under MIT for developers who want to self-host; self-hosted builds do not include this commercial license.
